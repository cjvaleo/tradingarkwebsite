— FAQ —

Questions beforeyou join.

Do I need experience to join?

No. Premium is built for traders at every level — from people opening their first chart to those refining a working process. The education library walks you through the model step by step, and the community is built around shared learning.

How much time do I need to commit per week?

As much or as little as you want. Live calls run 1–2 times a day during market hours, but everything is recorded. Many members watch recaps in the evening. The weekly education session is roughly 60–90 minutes. The minimum viable rhythm is a couple hours a week — the more you put in, the faster it compounds.

Is this signals or education?

Education. We don't sell entry alerts. We teach you the framework — market structure, setups, risk management, execution — so you can read the market yourself. Live calls show the framework in action, but the goal is always to make you self-sufficient, not dependent.

What's the cancel and refund policy?

Cancel anytime, no questions asked. Whop handles billing — you'll have one-click access to manage or cancel your subscription. Refunds are handled per Whop's policy; reach out and we'll do right by you.

What's the Rewind Journal and do I get full access?

The Rewind Journal is a full trading journal platform — track every trade with context, confluence, entries, exits, and outcomes. It's normally a paid tool on its own, but Premium members get full access included. Built into the community so you can share reviews and learn from how others are thinking.

How is this different from other trading communities?

Most communities sell you a feed of someone else's trades. We teach you the process behind the trades — so when the market changes, you don't get stranded. Less Discord noise, more structured learning. Less ego, more accountability. We're building traders, not followers.
